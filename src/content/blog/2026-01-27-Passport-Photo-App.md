Having a good passport photo is essential for travel documents. Traditional passport photo booths can be inconvenient and often produce subpar results. Enter the [Passport Photo App](https://passportphoto.app/), an AI-powered solution that allows users to create compliant passport photos from the comfort of their own home.

This will be completely free and ad based or buy me a coffee style donations. So many apps have hidden fees or subscriptions.

## How it works

Use your mobile phone to take a photo against a plain background. The app will have a simple interface to guide you thru the process and visual queues to ensure your face is properly aligned and lit.

AI is not required by the app.

The user can select the country and document type (passport, visa, ID card etc) and the app will automatically crop and adjust the photo to meet the specific requirements eg resolution min and maxes.

The app should support IOS and Android devices, and also support mac and windows pc's that have a web cam.
